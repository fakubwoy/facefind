Pricing FAQs

Common questions about plans, billing, and limits.

Can I switch plans later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades apply immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle, so you always get what you paid for.
I already have an account — which plan am I on?
All existing accounts have been automatically placed on the Starter (Free) plan. Your current datasets are safe and untouched. You can keep using FaceFind for free within those limits, or upgrade at any time to access higher limits.
What is the 4K compression on the free tier exactly?
Only images with a resolution higher than 4K (3840 × 2160 px) are resized down to 4K on upload. Images already below 4K are stored as-is, with no quality change. Face recognition accuracy is fully maintained at 4K. On all paid plans, your original files are stored unchanged.
Can I delete my dataset and upload a new one on the free plan?
Yes — there is no restriction on how often you do this. Delete your current dataset from the dashboard at any time and your upload slot is immediately freed. Upload a new one whenever you like. This makes the free plan perfectly usable for one-at-a-time personal events.
What's the difference between Personal and Photographer plans?
Personal plans are for personal, non-commercial use — using FaceFind for your own events, family gatherings, and reunions. Photographer plans include a commercial use licence, meaning you can offer FaceFind-powered photo delivery as part of your paid photography services: weddings, corporate events, sports coverage, concerts, and more.
How does annual billing work?
Annual billing charges a single upfront payment covering 12 months at a discounted rate — roughly equivalent to getting 2–3 months free compared to monthly billing. Renewals are automatic unless you cancel before the renewal date. You can see exact annual prices by toggling the billing switch at the top of this page.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept UPI, all major debit and credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), net banking, and popular wallets. All payments are processed securely in INR (₹). GST invoices are available on Photographer plans.
What happens to my data if I downgrade?
Your data is retained for 30 days after downgrading, giving you time to export or reactivate. Datasets over your new plan's limit are flagged but not deleted until after that grace period. You'll always receive a warning before any deletion happens.
