Common questions

Everything you need to know about plans and billing.

I already have an account — which plan am I on?
All existing accounts have been automatically placed on the Starter (Free) plan. Your datasets are safe and untouched. You can keep using FaceFind for free within those limits, or upgrade at any time.
What exactly is 4K compression on the free tier?
Only images with a resolution above 4K (3840 × 2160 px) are resized down on upload. Images already at or below 4K are stored exactly as uploaded — no quality change. On all paid plans, originals are stored without any compression.
Can I delete my dataset and upload a new one on the free plan?
Yes — there is no restriction on how often you do this. Delete your current dataset from the dashboard and your upload slot is freed immediately. Upload a fresh one whenever you like. This makes the free plan perfectly workable for one-event-at-a-time use.
What is the difference between Personal and Photographer plans?
Personal plans are for non-commercial use — your own family events, reunions, and gatherings. Photographer plans include a commercial use licence, meaning you can offer FaceFind-powered photo delivery as part of your paid photography services.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes. Upgrades apply immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle so you always get what you paid for.
How does annual billing work?
Annual billing charges a single upfront payment for 12 months at a discounted rate — roughly 2 months free compared to monthly. Renewals are automatic unless cancelled before the renewal date.
What payment methods are accepted?
UPI, all major debit and credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), net banking, and popular wallets. All payments are in INR. GST invoices are available on Photographer plans.
What happens to my data if I downgrade?
Your data is retained for 30 days after downgrading. Datasets over your new plan's limit are flagged but not deleted until after that grace period. You will always receive a clear warning before any deletion happens.
