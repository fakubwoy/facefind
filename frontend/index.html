FAQ

Got questions?

Everything you need to know about delivering photos with GotchaPic.

How do guests find their wedding photos?
You upload the full wedding gallery to GotchaPic, then share the link. Guests simply upload a selfie, and our AI instantly shows them every photo they appear in. No browsing, no tagging, no account needed for them.
Can I add my logo or studio name?
Absolutely. All paid plans let you add a custom watermark to photos. Studio Pro includes branded share links with your logo, turning every gallery into a premium client experience.
Do guests need to sign up or download an app?
No. Guests receive a share link and can upload their selfie and search immediately — no sign-up, no login, no app download. The search works in any modern web browser on phone or desktop.
What photo formats are supported?
GotchaPic accepts JPEG, PNG, WEBP, and BMP for both the photo collection and guest selfies. Upload your collection as a ZIP file — flat or organised in folders, both structures are supported.
How do I get started as a photographer?
Create a free account, upload your photo collection, wait for face indexing to complete (a few minutes for most event collections), then click Generate Share Link. Send that link to your guests and they can start finding themselves immediately. Upgrade anytime for watermarking, branding, and higher limits.
