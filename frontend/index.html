How it works

Three steps.
Zero browsing.

Set up a searchable photo collection in minutes. Guests find themselves without any help from you.

Step 01
Upload your photo collection

Drop in a ZIP of your event photos. FaceFind detects every face and builds a searchable index. Works with any size collection — a few hundred to tens of thousands of images.

Step 02
Generate a share link

One click creates a unique link for your collection. Share it via WhatsApp, email, or however you communicate with guests — no app download, no account required to use it.

Step 03
Guests upload a selfie

Anyone with the link can upload a photo of themselves. FaceFind searches the entire collection in milliseconds and shows every image they appear in, ready to view and download.

FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How do I find myself in wedding photos? +
The photographer uploads all wedding photos to FaceFind and generates a share link. Guests visit the link, upload a single selfie, and FaceFind uses AI face recognition to instantly find every photo that guest appears in — no browsing through thousands of images, no manual tagging required.
How does the face search actually work? +
FaceFind uses InsightFace (ArcFace model), a state-of-the-art face recognition system, to generate a unique numerical fingerprint for every face detected in your photo collection. When a guest uploads a selfie, their fingerprint is compared against all stored fingerprints using FAISS vector search — returning matches in under one second regardless of collection size.
Is face data stored? Is it private? +
FaceFind is self-hosted — all photos and face embeddings stay on the server you control. Nothing is sent to third-party cloud services. Guest selfies are used only to run the search and are not stored afterwards. You retain full ownership and control of all data.
Do guests need to sign up or download an app? +
No. Guests receive a share link and can upload their selfie and search immediately — no sign-up, no login, no app download. The search works in any modern web browser on phone or desktop.
How many photos can it search through? +
FaceFind uses FAISS vector search which handles tens of thousands of faces in under 5 milliseconds. Collections with 50,000+ photos are supported. Search speed stays fast as the collection grows.
What photo formats are supported? +
FaceFind accepts JPEG, PNG, WEBP, and BMP for both the photo collection and guest selfies. Upload your collection as a ZIP file — flat or organised in folders, both structures are supported.
How do I get started as a photographer? +
Create a free account, upload your photo collection as a ZIP, wait for face indexing to complete (a few minutes for most event collections), then click Generate Share Link. Send that link to your guests and they can start finding themselves immediately.
