FAQ

Got questions?

Everything you need to know about delivering a magical photo experience with Lenstagram.

How do guests find their photos?
You simply upload the gallery to Lenstagram and share a link. Guests take a quick selfie, and our smart technology instantly gathers every memory they appear in. No endless scrolling, just pure joy.
Can I add my logo or studio name?
Absolutely. All paid plans let you add a subtle, custom watermark to photos. Studio Pro includes beautifully branded share links with your logo, turning every gallery into a premium experience.
Do guests need to sign up or download an app?
Not at all. Guests receive a link and can upload their selfie to start searching immediately. There are no sign-ups, no logins, and no apps to install. It works beautifully right in their mobile browser.
What photo formats do you support?
We support all standard high-quality formats (JPEG, PNG, WEBP, BMP). Simply drop in your folders of photos, however you've organized them, and we'll handle all the heavy lifting.
How do I get started as a photographer?
Create a free account, upload your beautiful collection, and give us a few moments to softly organize the gallery. Then, generate your share link. Send it to your clients, and watch them light up as they find their photos. You can upgrade anytime to add your personal branding.
