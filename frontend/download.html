Quick start after download
  1. 1 Unzip the archive and run ./lenstagram start (Linux / Mac) or lenstagram.exe start (Windows)
  2. 2 Paste your license key when prompted, or set LENSTAGRAM_KEY=FF-XXXX-… as an environment variable before launching
  3. 3 Open http://localhost:8000 — your full dashboard is ready locally on your machine
  4. 4 Upload event galleries. All face recognition runs on your hardware; no images are sent to our servers
